My Philosophy

When you are learning the martial arts, discipline needs to be the ends, not the beginning, the goal and not the means. The more you try to use discipline to achieve something else, the shorter you will come with regards to your goals. Fortunately, I can help you. I’m THETAEKGUY, and I am a personal trainer specializes in Taekwondo, boxing, kickboxing, and general fitness. A black belt in Taekwondo and a Certified Instructor in kickboxing, I have trained and guided many students to overcome great challenges in their lives, and it is deeply fulfilling to me to watch people move through my lessons and gradually to come to know now just the practice better but themselves better.

How It All Started

When I was fourteen, I started training in Taekwondo in earnest. Fitness and exercise are my great passions in life, and anytime I am breaking a sweat and working out my body, you can be sure that I am smiling big and wide on the inside. Teaching, of course, is also near and dear to my heart, which makes the work that I do as a coach and trainer a confluence of my great loves. I enjoy every moment that I spend passing on Taekwondo, boxing, and kickboxing techniques. When you train with THETAEKGUY, you know that you are getting a
full-package, well-rounded program that includes workouts, techniques, and mental conditioning.

I Need Training!

Learn Self-Defense, Learn Yourself

As you learn self-defense, you learn much more than just a set of moves or a fitness routine: you learn about yourself. That is what the practice of martial arts is all about, and when you study with THETAEKGUY, you know that you are getting an education that is all about you and your place in the world. Come see what we can show you! We offer lessons in Taekwondo, boxing, and kickboxing, and all our self-defense classes feature basic moves both both Taekwondo and kickboxing, getting you ready for whatever you may come upon along your journey through the world.
